# Canonical Eras
[ example ⚠️ not canon ]

This is the master list of all established time periods in the Planescape West Marches campaign.

1.  **The Distant Past (Bookend Start)**
    * **Era 1: The First Faction War** (Approx. -500 to -300 PC)
        * *A time of initial chaos as the major factions formed and fought for control of Sigil's politics and portals.*
2.  **The Recent History of Sigil**
    * **Era 2: The Silent Conflict** (Approx. -50 to 0 PC)
        * *A tense period defined by espionage, cold war tactics, and the disappearance of the Lady of Pain for a cycle, leading to the current instability.*
3.  **The Present Day (Bookend End)**
    * **The Time of Active Exploration** (0 PC - Present)
        * *The period during which the player characters begin their adventures, exploring the open questions and frontiers of the multiverse.*
