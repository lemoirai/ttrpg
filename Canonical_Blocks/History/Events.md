# Canonical Events

This is the master list of all specific, resolved incidents from the past. These are indisputable facts.

**Events in Era 1: The First Faction War**
* **The Great Faction Divide:** The Bleak Cabal officially split from the Dustmen, with each taking control of a specific ward in Sigil.
* **The Treaty of Unfolding:** The Prince of the Githzerai signed a treaty with a rogue faction of the Guvners, granting the Guvners limited, temporary access to a fortress on Limbo.

**Events in Era 2: The Silent Conflict**
* **The Great Portal Sealing:** Due to an unknown planar contagion, the Lady of Pain sealed all major outbound portals from the Hive for one full year.
* **The Secret Relocation:** In the year before the campaign started, Factol Tethys of the Harmonium splinter faction secretly moved the Covenant Screed to a hidden manor on Mechanus, fearing a raid from the Mercykillers. (Resolved by adventure: OH-01)
